# The Bobiverse — Starmap & Chronicle

An interactive, fan-made companion to Dennis E. Taylor's **Bobiverse** series, covering books 1–4
(*We Are Legion (We Are Bob)*, *For We Are Many*, *All These Worlds*, *Heaven's River*).
No spoilers for *Not Till We Are Lost*.

**[🌌 Open the starmap](https://YOUR-PAGES-URL/)**

## Features

- **Starmap** — a 3D rotatable map of every significant star system, placed at real J2000
  astronomical coordinates, with Bob expansion routes, colony fleets, and the Others' harvesting
  paths. A year scrubber (2133–2334) replays the expansion of the Bobiverse.
- **Guided tours** — narrated camera journeys: Bob-1's Odyssey, Mario & the Hunt for the Others,
  the Human Exodus, and the Medeiros Wars.
- **Timeline** — ~50 dated events from Bob's death in 2016 to the accord with ANEC in 2334,
  cross-linked with the map.
- **Bob family tree** — the full clone genealogy with creators, generations, fates, and book-4
  factions, with its own time scrubber.
- **Factions & lore** — the Deltans, the Others, the Quinlans, the Pav, Medeiros, VEHEMENT, FAITH.

Everything is a single self-contained `index.html` — no build, no dependencies.

Data compiled from the books and the [Bobiverse wiki](https://bobiverse.fandom.com).
This is an unofficial fan project; all characters and settings belong to Dennis E. Taylor.
